About Tatu City

Africa's Most Ambitious New City

Tatu City is a 5,000-acre new city with homes, schools, offices, a shopping district, medical clinics, nature areas, a sport & entertainment complex and manufacturing area for more than 250,000 residents and tens of thousands of day visitors.

Kenya's first operational Special Economic Zone — a project of Rendeavour, Africa's largest urban land developer — providing reduced corporate taxes, zero-rated VAT and import duty exemptions.

Tatu Reach

Building Community With Purpose

Tatu Reach is Tatu City's ESG programme — benchmarked against the UN SDGs across four pillars: Education, Healthcare, Community, and Environmental Conservation. Since 2016, over 2 million meals funded for underserved schoolchildren.

📚
Education

Supporting 6 government schools with free meals, materials, uniforms and skills training for 500+ academy graduates.

🏥
Healthcare

Curafa clinic offering affordable healthcare from KES 100 consultation with annual coverage at KES 1,500.

🌿
Conservation

2,000+ indigenous trees in Tatu Bustani nursery. Rewilding hundreds of acres of native flora and fauna.

👩‍💼
Women's Empowerment

RenWoman initiative promoting gender equality across all Rendeavour markets in Africa.
